About MARSH

MARSH is a meme coin and NFT ecosystem built on Solana to support crocodile conservation. The project includes 100 exclusive Genesis NFTs that will be airdropped to holders.

The Hall of the Swamp contains 100 Crocodile Commanders divided into 5 Realms. Each Realm has 19 Commanders and 1 Lord. Total: 95 Commanders + 5 Lords = 100 Genesis Crocodiles.

Revenue from MARSH is dedicated to purchasing meat and supporting crocodile habitats.
